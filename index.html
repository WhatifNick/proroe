Pro Roe Virtual 5k

This event is organized by three siblings living in Indiana, Texas, and Australia who believe that each person should have the basic right to make decisions about their own body.

Indiana legislators plan to restrict abortion access in a special session set for July 25 at the Statehouse. ACLU of Indiana is organizing a rally outside of the Statehouse on the 25, urging “We cannot afford to stay quiet when our rights are on the line.”

Join us on July 24 for a virtual 5K run/walk to help raise money for abortion rights.
